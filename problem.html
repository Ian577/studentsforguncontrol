Guns are the leading cause of death in kids

In 2020 guns became the leading cause of death in children and teens. Previously the leading cause of death had been car/motor accidents making up 15.5% of deaths. As of 2021 guns are responsible for 18.7% of all deaths in children and teens.

Guns are the most common way to commit suicide

Every year an estimated 48,183 people take their own lives. Of the 48,183 people, 26,328 people used a gun. That is more than half. This is due to how accessible they are. Research done by the CDC shows that states with the least gun restrictions, have the highest gun suicide rates...

Passing stronger gun restrictions could prevent more than half of all suisides.
